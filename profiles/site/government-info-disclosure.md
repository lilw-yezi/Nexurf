## Platform Type
- Government information disclosure portals and public-information catalogues.

## Content Carrier Pattern
- Content may be split between index pages, catalogue pages, detail pages, and attachments.
- Metadata fields are often visible even when full text is in a document resource.

## Effective Entry
- Start from official domain and original detail page.
- Preserve document number, issuing department, release date, source, category, and effective status.
- Inspect attachments and viewer buttons.

## Known Traps
- Index page title may duplicate detail title but omit body.
- Attachment URLs may be relative or generated by JavaScript.
- Some files are hosted on separate department subdomains.

## Parameter Preservation Rules
- Preserve article IDs, channel IDs, and file IDs.
- Preserve source-site links and interpretation links.

## Extraction Notes
- Extract metadata first.
- Then resolve body carrier through HTML/article body, attachment list, viewer, or button resource.
